# Business Analysis Portfolio

## Overview
This repository serves as a professional portfolio of business analysis projects demonstrating end-to-end analytical capabilities across requirements management, process improvement, and data-driven decision support.

## Portfolio Objectives
- Elicit, analyze, and document business and functional requirements  
- Evaluate existing processes to identify gaps, risks, and improvement opportunities  
- Translate business needs into actionable insights and solution recommendations  

## Core Competencies
- Requirements Gathering & Documentation (BRD, FRD)
- Stakeholder Analysis & Communication
- Process Mapping (As-Is / To-Be)
- Gap Analysis & Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
- User Stories & Acceptance Criteria
- Business Process Improvement
- Agile & Scrum Fundamentals

## Tools & Techniques
- Documentation & Analysis: MS Excel, MS Word, Google Workspace  
- Data & Reporting: Excel (Advanced), SQL (Basic), Tableau  
- Collaboration & Delivery: JIRA, Confluence  
- Process & UX Mapping: Figma, Miro  

## Projects Included

### Hospital Management System – Business Analysis Case Study
- Analyzed end-to-end hospital workflows including patient registration and operations  
- Documented functional and non-functional requirements  
- Created As-Is and To-Be process flows to improve operational efficiency  

### Canteen Online Ordering System – Business Analysis Project
- Identified limitations in manual ordering processes  
- Defined business requirements for a digital ordering solution  
- Recommended features to enhance user experience and turnaround time  

### Employee Performance Management – Business Case
- Reviewed existing performance tracking mechanisms  
- Identified reporting gaps and inefficiencies  
- Proposed data-driven improvements for performance monitoring
